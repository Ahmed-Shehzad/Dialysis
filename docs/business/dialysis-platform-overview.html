The problem we set out to solve

Running a clinic today means juggling disconnected tools

😣 The everyday reality

  • Front desk, treatment notes, and billing all live in separate places
  • The same patient details get re‑typed again and again
  • Billing is manual, slow, and easy to get wrong — cash comes in late
  • Sharing records with other clinics is faxes, phone calls, and waiting
  • Proving you handled patient privacy correctly is a scramble

✨ What we built instead

  • One connected system the whole clinic shares
  • Enter the patient once — their details follow them everywhere
  • Turn a completed visit into a submitted insurance claim in one click
  • Share records securely with partner organisations, with consent
  • Privacy, security, and a full record of “who did what” built in
The solution, in one sentence

Everything a dialysis clinic does in a day — booking, treating, recording, billing, and sharing — in a single, joined‑up system.

Before

Billing staff manually pull together each insurer’s claims for the period — hours of work, easy to miss items, and money arrives late.

Now

One click assembles the claims, submits them, and reports exactly what went out — automatically. Less admin, fewer errors, faster cash flow.

A concrete example of the “do it once, do it right” idea running throughout the platform.
